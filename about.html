About Arya's Movie Musings - and the site owner

Hi movie people, enthusiasts, lovers, cinephiles...whatever, it's too long a list. My name's Arya and I'm the massive movie buff behind this little area of the web. When I'm not watching movies, discussing movies, or going down the rabbit hole analyzing movies, you'll find me here scribbling down my own opinions ranging from a huge range of stuff from classic films to movie franchises to b-grade movies, as well as movies that I love - and find and discover too

Why I made this site

  • Love of Story-telling: Movies are one of my favorite ways to escape, laugh, cry, and sometimes yell at the screen.
  • Sharing Everything: Everyone around me got tired me breaking down and analyzing movies and the characters in the movies to the absolute bit and hearing fan theories so now the internet gets them instead.

What you'll find here

  • Movie Reviews: Both quick and long in case you want something on the go or the full on musings and analysis and theorizing
  • Arya's Top 20 List: Because ranking stuff is fun!
  • Movie News: Keep up over what's happening.
  • Best and Worst: Seeing which is the worst and best - monthly and weekly - is fun as well!

Thanks for reading!

- Arya
